Last Letters

A journey through loss, space and memory. This film by Nils Clauss commemorates the victims of the tragic Sewol ferry accident, in which 304 out of 476 passengers and crew members died in 2014.

This Island is Ours (Trailer)

A feature documentary about the territorial dispute between Japan and Korea over the ownership of Dokdo/Takeshima islets. My role was as sound editor, sound mixer, and music composer.
