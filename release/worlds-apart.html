Worlds Apart

Jeremy Redmore and Levi Patel

Two New Zealand artists from completely different musical worlds have combined forces to create a stunning and unique collaboration. The project begins with Worlds Apart, a song that perfectly captures the sonic collaboration between this unlikely pairing.

"There are places this project took my voice to that it had never been before and that, combined with purposefully-challenging songwriting methods, has produced a unique cluster of songs I am so happy to share with the world," Redmore says.
