Music Composer | Producer | Engineer

Hello, my name is Levi Patel and I love to create music here in New Zealand and share it with you.

I create instrumental music that typically combines acoustic recordings with electronic manipulation. In addition to my own music, I compose for film and television, produce and engineer music for other artists, and design and mix sound for films. I'm also building a robot.

Thank you for visiting and I hope you'll enjoy what you find.
