Music Composer | Producer | Engineer

Hello, my name is Levi Patel and I love to create music here in New Zealand and share it with you.

I create instrumental music that typically combines acoustic recordings with electronic manipulation. In addition to my own music, I compose for film and television, produce and mix music for other artists, and provide audio engineering and sound design services. I'm also building a robot.

Thank you for visiting and I hope you'll enjoy what you find.

Through Winter Eyes - Music Video

Through Winter Eyes is an intimate piano and cello duet from my new album A Shifting Lightness, featuring a breathtaking cello performance and accompanied by this beautiful video by Jonathan Zsofi, Yonoko, Josh Allen, and their team.

Featured Project - Last Letters

Last Letters is a journey through loss, space, and memory. This short documentary by Nils Clauss commemorates the victims of the tragic Sewol ferry accident, in which 304 out of 476 passengers and crew members died in 2014.
