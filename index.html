Music Composer | Producer | Songwriter

Hello, my name is Levi Patel, and I love to create music here in New Zealand to share with you.

I create beautiful music, typically by capturing delicate instrumental sounds and shaping them with ethereal electronics. Besides my own music, I produce and engineer for other artists, co-write songs, and compose music and sound for film and television. I'm also building a robot.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you'll enjoy what you find.
